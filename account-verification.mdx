---
title: "Account Verification"
description: "Understand sender number ownership and Sinjapp account verification."
---

There are two separate checks before a sender number can send messages:

1. Sender number ownership is confirmed with OTP.
2. The sender number owner completes Sinjapp account verification.

Accepting the OTP proves that the tenant owns the sender number. Sending customer messages still requires the sender number owner account to be verified in Sinjapp.

## If The Account Is Not Verified

The sender number may appear as:

```text
pending_account_verification
```

The tenant does not need a new OTP. The sender number owner must complete Sinjapp account verification, then the number becomes ready for messaging.
